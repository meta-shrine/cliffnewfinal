Terms & Conditions

BOOKING TERMS AND CONDITIONS

The Cliff Pvt. Ltd is an adventure resort located at Kusma, Parbat, (57 Km from Pokhara). In addition to the thrilling adventure and adrenaline, The Cliff’s also offers glamping style accommodation with an option to stay in our wonderful family tents or our cozy deluxe tent with attached toilet and bathroom.

CANCELLATION POLICY

For new bookings our standard cancellation policy is; Cancellation more than 48 hours in advance will be refunded in full, Cancellation less than 48 hours in advance = 50% refund. If you fail to arrive for your scheduled booking time your booking will be non-refundable. Please check your activity details carefully for your report time. Full refund will be provided, if the jumpers aren’t able to jump due to technical issues or bad weather.

MEDICAL CONSIDERATIONS

Before you book, if you have any of the following; - High or low blood pressure - High myopia - Heart condition - Epilepsy - Broken or fractured bones/dislocations - Fragile skin - Neurological disorders - Diabetes - Asthma - Prosthetics - Panic/anxiety attacks - Any recent sprains/muscular injuries. Please note the above list does not automatically exclude you from our activities, and the list is not exhaustive. If you have concerns about a condition not mentioned, please contact our team prior to travel. Unfortunately, we can’t permit pregnant activity-seekers, or those with photosensitive epilepsy, have undergone recent surgery. And if you have bone and spine problems or a recent concussion, we’ll need to see a doctor’s certificate. If you wear glasses? For safety reasons, they may not be able to be worn, soft contact lenses are okay to be worn.

CLOTHING

Tight shoes and comfortable clothes must be worn while jumping. Jumping is not allowed while wearing flip flops, loose fitting shoes, barefoot or other loose items attached to your body.

BRIDGE ETIQUETTE

For safety reasons, jumpers are not allowed to take bag packs, cameras and mobile phones inside the capsule.

TIMING

Bungy jump is operated in groups as per weight category, we kindly request all the jumpers to allocate full day. Reporting time at the resort for the jumps are 9AM in the morning.

Our general weight limit is as below, but weight limit varies as per technical aspect, so please check with our office before booking

BungyMinimum 45 Kgs, Maximum 95 Kgs
Swing/TandemMinimum 35 Kgs, Maximum 130 Kgs
Age LimitParticipants must be at least 12 yrs old
