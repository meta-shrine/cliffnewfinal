Questions

I have questions about :

Is bungee jump safe?

Yes, the point of bungee is to procure out of this world excitement in a secured environment. We use internationally certified gears, that are checked before, during and after every jumper and every jumping session. We also make sure to train and evaluate our crew regularly. Each and every jumpers’ physical condition is verified in order to give you the most adventurous and safest experience possible.

Am I fit for bungee?

Each potential bungee jumper has to fill up a form mentioning the conditions which can present a risk for their safety (see “Terms & conditions). We urge our clients to be responsible and not hide any condition that could jeopardize their safety.

Am I fit for swing?

Each potential swing jumper has to fill up a form mentioning the conditions which can present a risk for their safety (see “Terms & conditions). We urge our clients to be responsible and not hide any condition that could jeopardize their safety.

What’s a bungee jump?

A bungee jump is an adventurous, adrenaline kicking activity in which the person is attached to a rubber, very dynamic rope (with an ankle harness) and jumps in a verticular way. After few seconds of free fall, the rope will reach maximum tension and pull the jumper back up (rebounce) and this for two or three times. Once the rebounces are over, the jumper is hoisted back up to the platform with a winch system.

What’s a giant ping?

A giant swing is an adventurous, adrenaline kicking activity in which the jumper is attached to a dynamic rope (through a full body harness, anchor point at waist level) and jumps in a pendular way. The swing offers a longer free fall than the bungee and somehow more of a flying than falling sensation. Once the pendular movement gets softer, the jumper is hoisted back up to the platform with a winch system.

What’s the difference between swing and bungee?

The main differences are : the rope, the anchor points and the jump. For more information, please refer to “What’s a bungee jump? And “What’s a giant swing?”

Age limit?

There’s no age limit for bungee and swing jumps, it is a question of physical condition (see “Terms & Conditions”) and weight (see “Weight limit?”)

Weight limit?

You will need to be minimum 38kg for both jumps and maximum 98kg for bungee and 128kg for swing.

Special gears/clothes?

Comfortable clothing (pants, no skirts) is recommended. Snickers or shoes that won’t fall during your jump. Glasses will need to be straped if you really need them for your jump but we recommend not to wear them as there is a chance of loosing them in action. Soft contact lenses only.

Do we need to book?

A booking 48 hours in advance is strongly advised as we often get packed.

How do I get my jump’s photos and videos?

Each and every jump is filmed, you can decide if you want your video before or after your jump (until ….after your jump)
